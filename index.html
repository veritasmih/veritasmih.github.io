Youngseok Song is a graduate student in Department of Statistics at Colorado State University since 2015. His research interests are high-dimensional inference, graphical model, and statistical genomics under the supervision of Prof. Wen Zhou. He received a BS in 2008, and MS in 2010 from Department of Mathematics at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), South Korea. He previously worked at KIS Pricing Inc. He was an organizer of Student Organized Activities and Research Seminars (SOARS) in CSU Stat department in 2017-18.
